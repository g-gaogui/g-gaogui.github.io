<h2>Overview</h2>

<p>
As AI systems become increasingly integrated into consumer products, ensuring
their reliability, robustness, and trustworthiness is critical for large-scale
deployment. This project developed ART (Availability, Robustness, and
Trustworthiness), a unified evaluation framework for systematically assessing
production AI models across multiple modalities, including text, speech, and
images. The framework was adopted across Tencent AI products and supported
model validation for applications serving more than 10 million users.
</p>

<h2>Technical Approach</h2>

<p>
The framework employed black-box adversarial evaluation techniques and
data augmentation strategies to simulate real-world distribution shifts and
stress-test model behavior. Evaluation protocols were designed to measure
robustness across diverse modalities while remaining model-agnostic, enabling
consistent assessment of heterogeneous AI systems deployed across Tencent's
product ecosystem. The resulting framework provided a scalable methodology for
identifying failure modes and improving model reliability prior to deployment.
</p>
