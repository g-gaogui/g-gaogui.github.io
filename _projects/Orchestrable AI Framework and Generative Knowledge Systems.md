<h2>Overview</h2>

<p>
As large language models become increasingly capable of automating complex
knowledge work, ensuring their outputs align with expert reasoning remains a
critical challenge. This project developed an orchestrable AI framework for
workflow automation and a Generative AI knowledge system that transforms public
financial information into structured reasoning artifacts. By integrating
expert feedback directly into the decision-making process, the system aimed to
improve transparency, confidence estimation, and the quality of AI-assisted
financial insights.
</p>

<h2>Technical Approach</h2>

<p>
The framework combines LLM-based orchestration with graph knowledge
representation to support multi-step financial reasoning. Public financial
information is transformed into structured knowledge graphs, enabling the
system to capture relationships between entities, events, and market signals.
To improve reliability, expert advisor feedback was incorporated into an
alignment mechanism that evaluates the logical consistency of generated
recommendations. The resulting Expert Alignment Score serves as a confidence
indicator by measuring how closely the AI's reasoning process matches
established expert decision flows.
</p>
